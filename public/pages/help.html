How BirdFeathers Works

BirdFeathers helps poultry farmers estimate feed requirements, production costs, and expected returns before starting a flock.

Steps

  1. Select poultry type
  2. Enter number of birds
  3. Enter feed market prices
  4. Review feed quantities
  5. Review projected revenue

Important Reminder

This calculator estimates feed and revenue only which comprises bulk of the cost outlay. Farmers should also plan for vaccines, medication, labour, housing, feeders, drinkers, heating, lighting, transport, and land costs.
